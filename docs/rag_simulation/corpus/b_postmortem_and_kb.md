# B4 Postmortem and Knowledge Base Workflow

## Postmortem Timeline

- Postmortem draft due: within `24 hours`.
- Final postmortem due: within `5 business days`.

## Required Postmortem Sections

- timeline
- root cause
- blast radius
- customer impact
- corrective actions

## Corrective Action Governance

- Every action must include `DRI` and due date.
- Overdue high-priority actions are reviewed weekly in engineering ops.

## Knowledge Base Conversion

- Confirmed root cause and fix procedure must be converted to KB article within `48 hours`.
- Recurring incident themes are reviewed monthly and linked back to KB updates.
